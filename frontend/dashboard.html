Help & Guidelines

Everything you need to use the portal effectively.

📅
Managing Reservations
Create reservations with guest name, contact, room type, and dates. The system auto-generates a unique number. Edit status via the table.
🧾
Billing & Receipts
Enter any valid reservation number for a full itemised receipt with 12% VAT calculated automatically. Use Print for a physical copy.
👥
Staff Management
Admins can create, edit, and deactivate staff accounts. Assign minimum required roles and deactivate when staff leave.
🔄
Status Workflow
Reservations: Confirmed → Checked In → Checked Out. Can be cancelled at any stage before checkout.
⬡
Dashboard Overview
Live snapshot of total, confirmed, and checked-in reservations plus active staff count and 8 recent entries.
🔒
Security & Sessions
Always sign out when leaving a workstation. Sessions expire on window close. Report suspicious activity to your admin.
